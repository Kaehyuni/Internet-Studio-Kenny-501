About Me

Hello, I am Kenny Nguyen and I am a senior. My major is ATEC General and I am working on a certification in UI and UX design with a minor in Audio Production. I want to earn an MFA and help a company grow with thoughtful design and creative social media. I already post my videos on YouTube and share my art on Instagram. I also want to become a professor and work with students. I enjoy guiding people through creative problems and learning from the different ways they think. Teaching helps me listen better, explain ideas clearly, and stay curious. I believe a supportive classroom gives students the confidence to try new things and build skills that last.
